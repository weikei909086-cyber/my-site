HOW TO USE (TL;DR)
--------------------------------
1) In the Pi Dev Portal, whitelist your domain:
   https://weikei909086-cyber.github.io
2) Upload `pi-auth.js` to the repo root (same folder as index.html).
3) Edit `index.html` and add, before </body>:

   <script src="https://sdk.minepi.com/pi-sdk.js"></script>
   <script src="./pi-auth.js"></script>

4) Make sure your sign-in button has id="pi-signin".
5) Commit and open your site in Pi Browser. Tap the button to authenticate.
